Hello

I’m Karl Tayeb, a 3rd year PhD student in the Committee of Genetics, Genomics, and Systems biology and the University of Chicago. I have the privilege of being advised by Matthew Stephens and Yoav Gilad.

This is my personal/research website. I’ve started this site with the goal of “working with the garage door open”. While the exact scope of what I post is still up in the air I aim for this site to contain a mix of research notes, teaching materials, and references posts.

I often find the notes that I take are helpful in the process of writing them (to organize my thinking, figure it out) but not after the fact. I hope that having an audience, if only imaginary, will encourage me to polish and organize my writing to a point where it can stand on it’s own.
